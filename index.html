// 01 — about

Nerds, Creatives & Dreamers is a small, curated evening in Berlin for two kinds of people who almost never end up in the same room: the ones who build things with code, and the ones who build things with imagination.

Developers and painters. Engineers and writers. Founders and photographers. In daily life these worlds rarely collide — here, that collision is the whole point.

Just a good space, a deliberate mix of curious people, and the freedom to let the evening unfold. Come ready to be surprised by who you meet and where the evening takes you.

We're here to socialize as human beings. And if you do exchange contacts — make it personal, not transactional.
